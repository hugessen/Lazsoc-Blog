Discover your passions

Our school is the perfect place to find who you are, who you want to become and discover what you enjoy most. With a low stake environment you will be able to explore all your interests learning from all the ups and downs along the way. Passion is the engine that drives the Lazaridis community and our community will help you find what you love. Whatever you may be interested in, LazSoc is here to help you dive into it.

Immerse yourself within an empowering community

With one of the smallest campuses in Ontario, the Lazaridis community is known to have the strongest and tight knit culture. Being a Lazaridis student, you are able to surround yourself with ambitious like minded students that push you past your comfort zone and help you pursue your passions. You get the oppurunity to inspire the lives of the generation that comes next by leading by example.

Experience what works best for you

Lazaridis students get a chance to see their work come to life. Focusing in on the why you get a chance to try new things in a hands on approach to see what works best for you. Through your experiences you will develop new skills and friendships that will last a lifetime. Putting your best foot forward, bring your ideas to life and leave behind that positive legacy.
